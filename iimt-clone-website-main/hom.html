School of Hotel Management, Catering & Tourism

About School

This school prepares students for leadership roles in hotel management, catering, tourism, and events. Students gain practical skills, internships, and exposure to the industry, ensuring global career readiness.

  • Bachelor of Hotel Management & Catering Technology
  • B.Sc. Home Science – Clinical Nutrition & Dietetics
  • B.Sc. Hotel Management
  • M.Sc. Home Science – Food & Nutrition
  • Master of Hotel Management & Catering Technology
  • Diploma – Food & Beverage Service
