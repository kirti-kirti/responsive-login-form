School of Life Science & Technology

About School

The School of Life Science and Technology (SoLST) offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Biotechnology, Microbiology, Botany, Zoology, and Biochemistry. The school focuses on research-led teaching, real-world impact, and developing skilled human resources.

Programs

  • B.Sc. (CBZ)
  • B.Sc. (Microbiology)
  • B.Sc. (Biotechnology)
  • M.Sc. (Biotechnology)
  • M.Sc. (Microbiology)
  • M.Sc. (Zoology)
  • Ph.D. (Biotechnology, Microbiology, Zoology, Biochemistry)
